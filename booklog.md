## Animal Farm - George Orwell - 02-05-2025 - 114 Pages
This is my first book. I have to say, I loved the speeches of the pigs trying to sway the preception of the other animals. Convincing them that Snowball was a traitor, making them question their memory. Gaslighting the animals, extended the 7 commandments and making it look like they were always that, gaslighting others to think their memory is corrupted. Having brainless sheeps chant “Four legs good, two legs bad” or later “four legs good, two legs *better*” to stop any arguments and not give room for debate or questions is soo good. There were moments while reading this when I paused and re-read a part cause the manipulation was soo good.
