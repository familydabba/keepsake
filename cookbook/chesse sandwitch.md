# Cheese Sandwich 

## Version 1 (8/10)
Add julien onion, garlic to the pan and stir fry them. After they are cooked, add milk to the pan. Add oregano, chilli flakes and salt as per your preference. Add 2-3 slices of chesse after the milk has boiled. Turn the gas off and let the cheese melt and create a nice, creamy, amalgamation.

Just put it on a slice and get to work munching.
