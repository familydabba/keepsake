# Skincare hacks

## Korean rice face-mask
Boil rice until mushy(overcooked), blend it, add a spoon of honey, mix-mix-mix. Apply it on the face for 15mins.

## Ice-water to the face
Dip your face in ice-water for 30sec. You’ll feel more awake, reduced puffiness, and increases happiness with yourself cause you are doing something for yourself.
